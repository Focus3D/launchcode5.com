Our Approach

Our team works directly with our clients in a rapid, collaborative & iterative process.

Working iteratively ensures that feedback can be quickly incorporated into the product & minimizes the cost of changes.

Launchcode5 was founded with seven founding principles that help to ensure the success of our projects and the positive relationships we have both with our clients and within our team:

Projects are defined in Measurable, Iterative Milestone
Delivering Quality is more important than Launchcode's Growth
Every Client is a Part of the Team
We Treat the Team like Family
Objectives, Reasoning & Expectations must always be clear
We are Dedicated to Innovation
Honesty & Integrity above all else
