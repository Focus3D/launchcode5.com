Our Culture & Principles

Clear principles help our clients understand us and foster those values in our staff. Every decision we make is filtered through this set of principles:

1.

Each Milestone Must Deliver Value

Every step of a project must deliver meaningful business value and projects must be structured such that, if cut short or cancelled, they still provide real business value to the client.

2.

Quality Over Growth

Launchcode will be better served by delivering quality to satisfied customers than by jeopardizing current projects or relationships in order to take on the next big thing.

3.

The Team is Family

We think for ourselves, speak our minds, say what needs to be said and help mentor each other.

We help each other master our respective crafts, actively promote & celebrate each other's accomplishments and prepare each other for future endeavours and challenges.

4.

Clients are a Part of the Team

Every project is a fluid undertaking that must be flexible enough to take advantage of new-found opportunities or to adapt to a changing business environment.

To ensure nimble decision-making, clients must be kept up-to-date through regular consultations & demonstrations.

5.

Expectations & Drivers Must be Clear

Understanding empowers team members to make intelligent design & development decisions.

Who? What? When? Where? and Why? Every team member should be able to answer these questions when starting a task.

6.

Dedication to Innovation

We dedicate time to experimenting with new technologies, to blog about them, to contribute to the open-source community and to share our experiences with our peers.

7.

Honesty & Integrity Above All Else

We expect every member of our staff to be a beacon to their team, our company and our clients. We believe that any time we cut a corner, use wiggle words, exaggerate our capabilities or do anything else even slightly unethical it undermines our integrity.
